About Blush & Bloom

Founder Story (FAKE)

Blush & Bloom was created as a fictional studio concept by founder Maya Ellis, who believed loc care should feel both technical and luxurious. The brand voice is warm and welcoming, while service structure is clear and professional. This template mirrors that balance so real salons can launch with confidence.

From first consultation through long-term maintenance, the studio emphasizes education, healthy growth, and realistic styling plans that respect each client’s lifestyle and schedule.

Mission

Deliver healthy-loc outcomes with soft glam polish and modern precision.

Values

Hair health over hype, communication over confusion, and consistency over shortcuts.
