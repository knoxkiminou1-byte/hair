Starter Loc Timeline

Week 1

Keep hands out of hair, sleep with satin nightly, avoid water-heavy workouts if possible.

Month 1

Expect frizz and slight unraveling in some sections. Stay consistent, don’t over-style.

Months 3–6

Budding phase starts. Texture changes are normal. Maintain your retwist schedule.

Year 1

Locs gain shape and density; refine routine with scalp-focused treatments.

Wash Schedule Guidance

Low activity

Every 3–4 weeks with lightweight cleanser.

Gym 3–5x/week

Every 2–3 weeks plus scalp refresh between washes.

Outdoor/high sweat

Every 10–14 days with clarifying support.

Night Routine

  • Satin bonnet or scarf nightly.
  • For longer locs: pineapple tie + loose bonnet.
  • For short locs: durag base + satin cap.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet locs.

Product Guide

Use

  • Low-residue shampoo
  • Water-based moisturizing mist
  • Light scalp serum/oil blend
  • Alcohol-free edge support (as needed)

Avoid

  • Heavy waxes and grease
  • Thick creams trapped at roots
  • Frequent product layering
  • Fragrance-heavy irritants
