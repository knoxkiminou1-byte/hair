What to book: quick decision guide

I’m brand new

Book consultation + starter loc install for method matching and timeline planning.

My roots need cleanup

Book wash + retwist. Add detox if you have visible buildup or dullness.

I have an event

Book retwist + style or bridal package 2–3 weeks in advance.

I have weak spots

Book repair consult first for a safe restoration route.

Policies

Deposit: 30% required for all new bookings. Late fee: $20 after 15 minutes. Cancellations: 24-hour notice required. Reschedules: one courtesy transfer with proper notice. No-show: deposit forfeited and future prepayment may be required.

Prep checklist

  • Arrive detangled and product-light.
  • Avoid heavy oils, waxes, butters, and gels before service day.
  • Bring inspiration photos with realistic expectations.
  • Disclose scalp concerns, allergies, or tension sensitivity in advance.
  • If booking install services, complete required consultation first.
